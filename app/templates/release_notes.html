    {% trans %}What's shakin' with {{ appname }}?{% endtrans %}

    v1.3.0 December 12, 2011

    • Moved hosting of CSS files to Google Cloud Storage. Note: This means you should update your embed tags.
      This change helps us accomplish three things:
      • Faster serving of CSS content – Serving CSS from Google Cloud Storage is 5-10 times faster than Google App Engine.
      • More reliable hosting – Google Cloud Storage is less likely to go down, and will not be affected by App Engine quotas
      • Lower costs. This is more for us than you, but it allows us to continue offering WebPutty for free longer.
    • Added auto-completion in the {{ appname }} editor. While you are typing, press Control-Space to activate auto-complete.
    • Enable line-wrapping in the {{ appname }} editor.
    • Added a blue chameleon to let you know when you've incorrectly used a relative link in your stylesheet.
    • Fixed a number of bugs, including a major bug in PySCSS that caused radial-gradients not to work in WebKit browsers.
